Snufkin

About Snufkin

Snufkin is the epitome of a seemingly peaceful queer anarchist.

Edited drawing of Snufkin saying "Be gay, do crime"

Characteristics

  1. He's a wanderer
  2. He despises authority
  3. He loves his friends but is ultimately an introvert and needs time alone
  4. He is Little My's half-brother
